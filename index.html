My name is Michael, I'm a software engineer skilled in full stack development. I have experience building RESTful APIs using both Node.js and Python, working with databases and ready-built APIs, and making responsive frontend UIs with React.js.

Eventually I decided I'd rather pursue my passion of working with technology, so I began to teach myself software engineering and found joy in the process.

I am pursuing a position as a Software Engineer where I can continue to challenge myself and learn new skills, all while working with knowledgeable teams to create amazing applications.
